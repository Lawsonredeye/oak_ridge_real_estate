About Oakridge Real Estate

We're more than a real estate agency — we're your neighbors, committed to building stronger communities one home at a time.

Our Story

Oakridge Real Estate was founded in 2010 by Michael Carter with a simple belief: buying or selling a home should be an exciting, transparent, and deeply personal experience — not a stressful transaction.

What started as a small two-person office in Nashville has grown into a trusted agency serving families across Tennessee and beyond. Over the past 15 years, we've helped more than 1,200 families find their dream homes and have closed over $500 million in property transactions.

Our secret? We treat every client like family. We listen carefully, act with integrity, and go the extra mile — whether that means a late-night showing, a detailed market analysis, or simply being available to answer questions whenever they arise.
